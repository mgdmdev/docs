## How It Works

1. **Partners publish events** when users take actions (read articles, make purchases, refer friends)
2. **The rewards engine** matches events to rules, calculates points with tier multipliers, and enforces caps
3. **Users earn points** that accumulate toward tier upgrades and can be redeemed for rewards or cashback
